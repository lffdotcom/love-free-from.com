Welcome to the Love Free From wiki. This is a community-based wiki to collect and share helpful information and bring shared joy in eating free from food, such as gluten-free, diary-free, oat-free.

Two places to look at is:

  • Information about specific free-from product and places to eat/order food in Locations and Places
  • Cooking related information at Cooking and Tips

If you want to contribute, please have a look at the Contributing Guide and if you want to get in touch with other members of the community check out Connect.
