Welcome to the Love Free From wiki. This is a community-based wiki to collect and share helpful information and bring shared joy in eating free from food, such as gluten-free, diary-free, oat-free, etc.

If you want to contribute, all you need is to login and you'll be able to add/edit content. Have a look Contributing Guide and if you want to get in touch with other members of the community check out Connect.

Two places to look at is:

  • Information about specific free-from product and places to eat/order food in Locations and Places
  • Cooking related information at Cooking and Tips

All content from this wiki is also backed up and available on GitHub.
